BBQ RIBS RECIPIE

Descripton

This smokey bbq rib recipie involves very little overall effort. Only needing a couple of ingreditents you are really able to set it and forget it with this great new recipie.
ingreditents
  • brown sugar
  • chili flakes
  • garlic powder
  • onion podwer
  • salt
Steps
  1. Combine all dry ingredints into a bowl
  2. Preheat oven to 225
  3. Cook for three hours
  4. serve hot and enjoy
