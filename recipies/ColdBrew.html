Cold Brew

This easy cold brew recipie is great for anybody who wants to have a nice cold coffe and stop a starbucks addiction. And it's easily modifiable

Ingredints
  • Cofee Grounds (of your choice)
  • French Press
  • Creamer or milk
  • Some kind of sweetner *I enjoy caramel and vanilla
Steps
  1. put as much coffe grounds as you want for the week into the french press
  2. Then add double the amount of coffe grounds amount in water IE: 1 cup grounds two cups water
  3. Let sit at least over night or longer to soak up the water and bring out the flavor
  4. Strain grains
  5. Enjoy
